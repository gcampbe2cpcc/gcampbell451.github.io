Project Milestone #1 - Website Topic Approval

The site I wish to create is one dedicated to the UNC-Chapel Hill men's basketball team and its history.

  • The purpose of the site is to pay tribute to the men's basketball program at UNC. I want the site to be a source of information and build excitement and interest about the program. In particular, the site will showcase the seasons in which the team won the National Championship.
  • The intended audience is the intersection of college basketball fans and people who have an allegiance to the University.
  • Content included in the site will be statistics both from the overall history of the program and from the individual seasons when the team won the National Championship. There is likely to be biographical information about coaches and perhaps some prominent players. I intend to give a synopsis of the title seasons, and include pictures of players and coaches if I can do so without copyright issues.
  • Similar or related sites include goheels.com, tarheeltimes.com, and oldnorthbanter.com.
