Project Milestone 2-Site Planning Analysis

Web Site Goal

I want the site to be a tribute to one of my favorite Earthly entities: the men's basketball program for UNC-Chapel Hill. It will hopefully be a place where any interested party can get basic historical information about the program and its players/coaches. In particular I will highlight the seasons where the program won the National Championship.

Working Title of Pages

  • Home

    • This page will mainly have historical information. I imagine an overview section and a separate statistical section.

  • 1924

    • Each of the pages corresponding to a year will have the game-by-game record floating on the left and the roster floating on the right. Each will have their position fixed. In the center element I will write a synopsis of the season.

  • 1982

This page will enable users to contact me with corrections or additions to the material presented. Perhaps there will be a comments section.

Information Needed

Team Statistics
I likely will get most of the statistics from Wikipedia. If needed, I'm certain there are official sites with more detailed information.
Graphics/Photos
Hopefully there are free-use photos of the players/coaches available. If not, I will try contacting the University for permission to use their media.
Anecdotal Information
I probably won't be able to find anyone who remembers the 1924/1957 seasons, but I will poll friends and family as well as draw on my own memories when writing synopses of the title seasons.
